About Us

Jka/Wf Kenya

Jka/Wf Kenya is a non profit making association based in Kenya and is associated with the Japan Karate Association (“JKA”) in Japan, one of the most prestigious, oldest and largest Shotokan Karate organisations in the world. Our training for martial arts and karate has it’s roots in the teachings of Sensei Ochi and the great masters of the JKA in Japan.we follow the five basics of Gichin Funakoshi: character, sincererity, effort, etiquette and self control.

Who are the Jka?

The Japan Karate Association (JKA), is the world’s largest and most prestigious karate organization. Their mission is to promote the way of karate throughout the world, while ensuring that it remains true to the philosophical precepts since its establishment .The JKA is the generally-recognized heir to the Shotokan karate tradition as developed by Supreme Master Funakoshi Gichin, and teaches a specially-refined form of Shotokan style karate which puts heavy focus on kihon (basic techniques), kata (forms), and kumite (sparring) to develop a range of powerful and dynamic techniques.

History

Originally, the martial art Te (“Hand”) developed in Okinawa as a system of self-defense. In 1868 of Okinawan karate master Funakoshi Gichin was born. He dedicated his whole life to promoting the values of the art, and introduced the way of karate-jutsu to Japan, where it spread across the country. By 1949, his followers had established an association for the promotion of karate; they called it Nihon Karate Kyokai, or Japan Karate Association. It was the beginning of the JKA…
